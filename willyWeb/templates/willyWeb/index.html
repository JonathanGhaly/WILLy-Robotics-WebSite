About Us

Why We Are Better

We spend more time with the students and make them build the robots and do programming after they learn with them selves.

Our vision

Our goal is for STEM Robotics Education to be recognized by the educational community as a national leader in program-based learning and STEM training.
Our students who graduate from our courses , have the desire to continue their studies in Science in the field of STEM.

Over time,Willy Robotics Academy will explore innovative teaching methods but will also seek collaborations with other Educational and Research bodies. Our learning space exudes an atmosphere that resembles a industry science lab and provides students with an environment where they can learn and create. In this space, Willy Robotics Academy hosts schools, where visitors learn from our trainers about STEM Science-Based Learning. Our laboratories have the latest educational technology (3D printers, Robotic Kits).

Our educational values

The following values represent the guiding principles we use in our Academy to achieve our goals:

  • Research
  • Contact
  • Cooperation
  • Creativity
Based on these four areas, Willy Robotics Academy will seek to provide learning opportunities based on project-based and hands-on training. We constantly appreciate the diversity of our departments and adapt our lessons to each student, in line with their interests and skills We support and encourage our students to create and develop expectations based on strengths or even weaknesses, different educational backgrounds and personalities.

Our educational goals

At Willy Robotics Academy we aim to create an innovative educational space, open to society, by cultivating an open collaborative culture.

Objective 1: Increase students' interest in science education covered by STEM, by creating and enhancing STEM-related curricula.

Objective 2: Provide opportunities for students to discover educational interests, explore their future career paths and create plans for their future.

Objective 3: Adopt an educational plan that supports student learning, well-being based on five key points: Persistence, Research, Communication, Collaboration and Creativity.

Objective 4: Continue to create and improve alternative assessments to measure student learning.
